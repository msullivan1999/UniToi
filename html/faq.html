Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do I need to sign up to use UniToi?

No, you don't, however, certain features such as reviews and reporting problems requires signing up for an account.

How do you report a problem with a toilet?

Simply search for the toilet and click on the "Report issue" button on the toilet information page.

How can I contact the UniToi developers?

Go to the "About us" page and click on the "Contact us" link.
